Safety is Our Priority

We enforce strict guidelines and use modern safeguards to ensure your conversations remain a pleasant and judgment-free experience.

Profile Verification

Before a user is highlighted on the platform or upgraded to a Mingle Star, their identity must pass our internal review process. This involves phone number verification and a quick selfie check to keep bots strictly out of Mingl.

1-Click Reporting & Blocking

If you ever feel uncomfortable, our robust reporting system is available right on the chat screen. You can block or report a user instantly. Our 24/7 moderation team investigates all reports thoroughly and takes immediate action.

Privacy Protection

Your real phone number and personal details are completely hidden from other users. You only share what you put on your public profile. Voice and text chats are routed through our secure servers directly.

Community Guidelines

Be kind. No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or explicit content. Mingle is a place to learn, connect, and enjoy engaging conversation. Violating these rules results in a permanent ban.
